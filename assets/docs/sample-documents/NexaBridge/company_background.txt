ABOUT NEXABRIDGE TECHNOLOGIES

NexaBridge Technologies PLC is a mid-sized UK-based software consultancy and managed
services provider, founded in 2009 and headquartered in Manchester, with additional
offices in London and Edinburgh. The company employs approximately 420 staff and
serves clients primarily in the financial services, healthcare, and logistics sectors.

NexaBridge specialises in enterprise digital transformation, cloud migration, and
bespoke software development. Annual revenue for FY2024 was £47.2 million, representing
a 14% year-on-year growth.

CURRENT WORKFORCE CHALLENGES

Following a rapid hiring phase in 2022–2023, NexaBridge identified significant skill
gaps across the organisation, particularly in:

  - Modern cloud-native development (Kubernetes, microservices)
  - Data literacy and AI fluency across non-technical roles
  - Leadership and people management skills for newly promoted team leads
  - Client communication and commercial acumen in the Engineering division
  - Agile at scale (SAFe framework adoption is underway)

The CPO, Sandra Okafor, has mandated a structured Learning & Development initiative
for 2025, with a budget of £380,000 allocated for external training and consultancy.

The company has engaged Elevate Learning Consultants Ltd as its training partner
to conduct a Training Needs Analysis (TNA) and design a bespoke learning pathway
for three priority cohorts:

  Cohort A — Engineering & Delivery leads and senior engineers (approx. 60 people)
  Cohort B — Data & AI Practice staff across all levels (approx. 55 people)
  Cohort C — Client Services and Corporate managers (approx. 40 people)

TARGET OUTCOMES (as stated by CPO Sandra Okafor):

  1. Reduce dependency on contract staff by upskilling permanent engineers in cloud tech.
  2. Enable all staff to understand and responsibly use AI tools by Q3 2025.
  3. Improve client satisfaction scores (NPS) from current 42 to 60+ by end of 2025.
  4. Develop 12 high-potential employees for senior leadership roles over 18 months.
  5. Achieve Investors in People Gold accreditation by December 2025.
